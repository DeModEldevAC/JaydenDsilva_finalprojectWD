Preserve Goa’s Green Cover

Tackling deforestation in Mollem and Mhadei

The Issue

The Government of Goa has approved multiple infrastructure projects that cut through vital forests...

These projects include highways, railways, and power lines through protected areas like Mollem National Park. Local communities and environmentalists have protested, but approvals continue despite the irreversible ecological damage. Forest loss here affects wildlife corridors, water sources like the Mhadei river, and contributes to flooding and climate instability.
